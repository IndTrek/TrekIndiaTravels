Day-1 Activities

The Padmanabhaswamy Temple, located in Kerala, India, is renowned for its opulent architecture and religious significance, dedicated to the Hindu deity Lord Vishnu. It gained international attention due to the discovery of vast treasures hidden within its vaults, making it one of the wealthiest religious institutions in the world.

Alleppey, situated in the southern Indian state of Kerala, is renowned for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and vibrant houseboat cruises, offering visitors a tranquil escape amidst nature's beauty. Known as the "Venice of the East," Alleppey's picturesque canals and charming waterways provide a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers seeking relaxation and exploration.

Kerala's tea estates sprawl across verdant hills, creating a breathtaking landscape of lush greenery and misty valleys. These estates not only produce some of India's finest teas but also offer visitors a chance to witness the tea-making process firsthand while immersing themselves in the serene beauty of the Western Ghats.

Day-2 Activities

Munnar, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, captivates visitors with its sprawling tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, and pristine valleys. Known for its cool climate and stunning landscapes, Munnar is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike, offering opportunities for trekking, wildlife spotting, and rejuvenating amidst the tranquil surroundings.

Magic Planet Theme Park ,offers a thrilling array of rides, attractions, and entertainment for all ages, promising a fun-filled day with family and friends amidst colorful surroundings and joyful atmosphere

Shri Chitra Art Gallery, showcases a rich collection of Indian paintings, including traditional Tanjore and Mysore styles, as well as works by renowned artists like Raja Ravi Varma, providing a captivating journey through South Indian art and culture.

Day-3 Activities

Thiruvananthapuram Zoo , one of the oldest in Asia, is home to a diverse range of animals, including royal Bengal tigers, Indian elephants, and exotic bird species, offering an educational and entertaining experience for wildlife enthusiasts and families alike.

Kovalam Beach, renowned for its crescent-shaped coastline and golden sands, offers serene waters for swimming and water sports, along with vibrant beachside cafes and stunning sunset views, making it a favorite destination for tourists seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Vizhinjam Light House , perched atop a rocky cliff overlooking the Arabian Sea, offers panoramic views of the coastline and surrounding landscapes, making it a popular spot for photography enthusiasts and visitors seeking breathtaking vistas.
