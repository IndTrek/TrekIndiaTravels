Day-1 Activities

Hidimba Devi Temple, is a serene wooden temple dedicated to Hidimba, wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata, nestled amidst tall deodar trees, offering a tranquil atmosphere for worship and reflection.

Manali Nature Park ,provides a lush green escape with its dense forests, meandering paths, and diverse flora and fauna, perfect for nature enthusiasts and leisurely strolls.

Mall Road, the heart of Manali, buzzes with activity, offering a plethora of shops, eateries, and vibrant local culture, making it a favorite spot for both tourists and locals alike.

Day-2 Activities

Solang Valley, a haven for adventure seekers, offers thrilling activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing amidst breathtaking Himalayan landscapes.

Rohtang Pass, situated at an altitude of 13,050 feet, offers panoramic views of snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and pristine valleys, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking natural beauty and adventure.

Vashisht Hot Water Springs, and Temple are revered for their natural hot sulfur springs, believed to have healing properties, and the ancient temple dedicated to sage Vashisht, offering a spiritual retreat amidst picturesque surroundings.

Day-3 Activities

Naggar Castle, a historic marvel nestled in the hills overlooking the Beas River, showcases traditional Himachali architecture and art, providing insights into the region's rich cultural heritage.

Manu Temple, dedicated to the sage Manu, the progenitor of mankind according to Hindu mythology, offers breathtaking views of the valley and a serene ambiance for spiritual contemplation.

Van Vihar National Park, located along the banks of the Beas River, is a serene oasis with lush greenery, towering deodar trees, and peaceful walking trails, ideal for nature lovers and families seeking tranquility amidst nature's bounty.
